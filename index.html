The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.


Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

The political landscape of Bangladesh has long been dominated by the leadership of Sheikh Hasina, the country's longest-serving Prime Minister. Her leadership has shaped the nation's socio-economic trajectory, but as with any long tenure, the time inevitably comes for change. And when the change is so dramatic, it is vital to ensure stable leadership going forward. In envisioning the next chapter for Bangladesh Nobel Laureate, Professor Muhammad Yunus stands out as a potential leader who could navigate the country towards a more inclusive and sustainable future. Known globally as the "Banker to the Poor" and the pioneer of microfinance, Yunus has the credentials, vision, and integrity necessary to lead Bangladesh into a new era of prosperity and social justice.
